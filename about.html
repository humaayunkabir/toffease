Medical Physicians

Dr. Rupesh Chandra

Dr. Rupesh Chandra is a highly motivated and multi-skilled medical physician with 7 years of experience in renowned hospitals at center of excellence and healthcare management companies across the nation. He is a former Assistant Director at a reputed medical college in Dehradun, UK. He specializes in critical care management, as ICU Interventionist, OT procedures, central venous and arterial cannulations, and expertly manages cases in emergency medicine. Dr. Chandra also has experience in managing pain and sedation in critical care settings, handling emergency cases, and routine medicine cases.

Dr. Chandra's academic qualifications include an M.B.B.S from JJMMC, Davangere, and a M.D degree from prestigious Maulana Azad Medical College, New Delhi and done his residency from AlIMS, New Delhi. He is working as medicolegal specialist and having extensive experience in dealing with medicolegal cases. Dr. Chandra is a lifetime member of Medicolegal society of Delhi and registered with the DMC and NMC (formerly MCI). He holds certifications in ACLS and BLS from LNJP Hospital, New Delhi. He has also attended several conferences and workshops related to medical emergency management, legal issues in medical and dental practices, DNA technology and Role of DNA fingerprintings in crime and justice.

Ayurvedic Physician

Dr. Anshika Vishwakarma

Dr. Anshika Vishwakarma is a skilled Ayurvedic Physician with a passion for promoting health and well-being in the community. She has completed her Bachelor of Ayurvedic Medicine & Surgery (BAMS) from RDMC Ayurvedic College and Hospital and has additional qualifications from various organizations.

Dr. Vishwakarma has worked as a Senior Research Fellow (SRF) on the Non-Communicable Disease (NCD) Project for Lower Economic People under SCSP Programme led by AYUSH Ministry. She has also worked as a Resident Medical Officer (RMO) at the Labor Room, Labor OT, Post-Operative Ward in the OBG/GYN department at Rathi Hospital, Delhi.

Furthermore, she has extensive experience in conducting clinical trials and research, managing junior doctors, lab technicians, pharmacists, data entry operators, and MTAs. Dr. Vishwakarma is skilled in managing anaemia, gynaecological, and gastroenterological cases.

She has attended numerous conferences and has received several certifications, including the largest class of CHARAK SAMHITA, Niramaya, A approach to Women's health through Ayurveda, and many more. Dr. Vishwakarma also has a keen interest in promoting hygiene and good health.
